The CityGML Geometrical Transformation and Validation Tool (CityGTV) was developed at the "Institute of Energy Efficiency and Sustainable Building (e3D), RWTH Aachen University" under the supervision of Avichal Malhotra. 
This tool was developed for Python 3.5+ using PySide2. A detailed list of the required libraries can be found in the readme.
